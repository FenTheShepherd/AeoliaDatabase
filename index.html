The Astral Children

Born after a time where the peace of New Aeolia was established, these children were given the blessings of the three progenitors, who founded the lands a millenia ago.

Astral Princess

Clover von Aeolia

"If the goddess has favorites, why wouldn't that be me?"

The youngest triplet daughter of Fenjamin and Genny. An energetic girl with a heart of gold, yet fate had different plans for her.

Likes: Sunrises and Sunsets, Constellations, Sweets, Festivals.

Boon: ATK⮝, Luck⮝

Bane: HP⮟, DEF⮟

Bonus Fact: Clover is her homeland's favorite royal, and much of it comes to her kindhearted spirit and witty charm.

Lunar Heiress

Dyvia von Aeolia

"Sometimes I wonder... am I sufficient enough to walk in my father's footsteps?"

The eldest triplet daughter of Fenjamin and Genny, and current heir to the throne. Cold and aloof, yet cares deeply for her family.

Likes: Archery, Hearty Meals, Her own imposing presence.

Boon: DEX⮝, SPD⮝

Bane: ATK⮟, MAG⮟

Bonus Fact: As a child, Dyvia once accidentaly downed a whole bottle of imported ale from Genny's homeland, yet was completely fine despite the strength of the drink.

Solar Saint

Kirb von Aeolia

"Those two can be so wreckless sometimes... but what kind of brother would I be if I weren't there beside them?"

The middle triplet son of Fenjamin and Genny. A healer with incredible magical talent, yet feels uncomfident of his skillset.

Likes: Alchemy, sheep, his mother's pegasus.

Boon: MAG⮝, HP⮝⮝

Bane: ATK⮟, Luck⮟

Bonus Fact: Kirb is often mistaken for being female, and his higher pitched voice doesn't help convinve otherwise, yet he embraces his more feminine side.
